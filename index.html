Our Mission and Our Family

Generosity that is personal, thoughtful, and relational.

Our mission is to empower donors to make a direct and meaningful impact by providing targeted financial support to individuals, families, and foundations in need. We facilitate grants and scholarships to deserving recipients, fostering opportunities for growth and development. We also create a vibrant community where donors and beneficiaries can connect, share life experiences, and build lasting relationships.

The Henriksen family created this fund to make giving easier and more intentional. We saw people around us facing real financial and life challenges, and donors who wanted to help in ways that felt personal, not distant. What We Do

We focus our efforts where a relatively small amount of support can make a clear and meaningful difference. These are the main ways the Henriksen Family Giving Fund serves people and communities.

Direct Financial Assistance

Providing grants or scholarships to individuals, families, or nonprofit organizations that are facing financial hardship or pursuing meaningful opportunities.

Educational Programs

Offering educational resources, workshops, or seminars that equip recipients with skills and knowledge that can improve their life circumstances.

Community Building

Hosting gatherings, support groups, or online spaces where donors and beneficiaries can connect, share experiences, and offer mutual support.

Awareness and Storytelling

Creating and sharing materials that highlight specific needs and tell the stories of people who have been helped, encouraging others to give.

Fundraising

Organizing charitable events and campaigns, both online and in person, to raise funds that power our grants and scholarship programs.

Connections and Partnerships

Connecting recipients with additional resources, collaborating with other nonprofits, and building a network of partners.

Grants and Scholarships

We provide grants and scholarships that support real people with real needs. Every request is reviewed individually. We consider both financial need and the potential for lasting impact.

Types of Scholarships and Grants

  • Educational Scholarships. Financial support for students pursuing higher education.
  • Skill Development Scholarships. Grants for individuals seeking to build new skills through workshops.
  • Special Needs Scholarships. Assistance for individuals with disabilities to access resources.
  • Underrepresented Group Scholarships. Support to foster educational and personal growth.
  • Emergency Scholarships. Short term financial assistance for sudden hardship.
  • Continuing Education. Support for adults returning to school.
  • Merit Based with Financial Need. Recognition for strong achievements.

General Guidelines

  • Applications are accepted on a rolling basis.
  • We may request documentation (invoices, letters).
  • Funds may be paid directly to providers/schools.
  • Submitting an application does not guarantee funding.

For Donors

We help donors give with confidence, clarity, and connection. As a donor, you can support people and causes you care about while relying on our family board to steward funds thoughtfully.

Why give through the Henriksen Family Giving Fund

  • Personal Connection. Support individuals whose stories matter to you.
  • Thoughtful Review. Our board reviews each request manually.
  • Focused Impact. Directing funds toward clear needs.
  • Relational Giving. Creating ways to share updates and stories.
